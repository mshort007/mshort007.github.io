Welcome endurance riders! We are currently preparing for a multi-week mountain bike ride through forests, high deserts, and just about everything in between. That's right, it is time once again for the annual "Cycle the Continental Divide" event. If you have trained hard and have what it takes, then come prove yourself in this incredible ride. We look forward to meetting and riding with you!

We cannot be held responsible for the failure immigration and customs enforcement officers denying entry into the U.S. or Canada.
