Guided Tours

Welcome to our Guided Tours area. We have enthousiastic tour guides waiting to adventure and enjoy the outdoors with you. There are two tours currently offered. A Casual Tour and and Explorer Tour. The Casual Tour is aimed towards families and children, low impact excercise seekers, or people just wanted to get out and enjoy the outdoors without breaking too much of a sweat. The Explorer Tour is geared towards more experienced riders with a sense of adventure. This tour will take you offroad and into some areas that will challenge experienced riders. It is an intense workout and will provide a sense of accomplishment after completing this challenging tour.

Casual Tour

Skill Level: Beginners, Casual Riders
Distance: 2.1 miles
Cost: $11
Location: East Gallatin Recreation Area Departures: 10AM, 12PM, 2PM, 4PM Mon-Sat Duration: 1 hour & 15 minutes
Description: A slow scenic ride around flat and low grade hill areas with green meadows, trees, and flowers. Participants often see birds, butterflies, and an the natural beauty common to the Bozeman area. This route is perfect for beginners, families with small children, and casual riders. Choose this tour option if you are more interested in enjoying the scenery and having a peaceful relaxed ride.

Explorer Tour

Skill Level: Experienced Riders, Adventure Seekers
Distance: 5.6 miles
Cost: $19
Location: College M Trailhead and Picnic Area
Departures: 10AM, 2PM Thu-Sat
Duration: 3 hour & 35 minutes
Description: An intense ride through the rugged hills and wooded area trails of the Bozeman area. This tour is for experienced riders who can pedal up semi-steep hill areas and are capable of manouvering thier bike through trails that are narrow at times. This tour adventures through the backwoods and hillsides of Bozeman and will appeal to those seeking challenge and adventure.
