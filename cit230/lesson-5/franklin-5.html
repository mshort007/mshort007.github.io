Franklin Breaks Record

Franklin has broken the record for the warmest temperatures seen in years in the entire county. Our usual cold climate topping out at 79° F for the yearly high, ongoing for many decades, has finally given us a pleasant surprise with temperatures that make it wonderfully pleasant to be outdoors. Local clothing shops that have rarely had success selling shorts cannot keep them in stock. People are going outdoors and enjoying fun in the sun. Local parks have been found to be at their full capacity every day. There is no better time to live in Franklin than right now. The local school districts have even brought up the idea of taking spring break early in order to enjoy this miraculous weather. The last time 90° F was registered in Franlkin was July 17, 1937. If you have not had the chance to venture outside to enjoy the weather lately, please put it on your "to do" list. Who knows when weather this good will come again.
